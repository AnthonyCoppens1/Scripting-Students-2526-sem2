# SANTA PACKAGE
## LilElfCalculations
- helloSanta() Greets Santa
- helloElfName(name) Greets a little elf by its own name
- countStock(wrappedGifts, gifts) Calculates the amount of gifts left to wrap!
- KidsReceivedCoal(coalAmount) Generates a random amount of kids that have received the punishment of coal for Christmas.

## ChristmasLove
- ChristmasLove(name1, name2) Calculates the love % between 2 possibly happy people
- badLove(name1, name2) Calculates a terrible percentage of love
